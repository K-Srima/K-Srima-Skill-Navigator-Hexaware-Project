Help and FAQ

How do I register for the Skill Navigator platform?

To register, simply click on the "Sign Up" button on the homepage and fill out the registration form with your details.

Can I change my profile information or settings?

Yes, you can update your profile information and settings by accessing the "Profile" and "Settings" pages from the navigation menu.

How do I access my training progress and feedback?

You can view your training progress and feedback by navigating to the "Dashboard" and "Training Progress" pages.

Who can I contact for support?

If you have any questions or need assistance, you can reach out to our support team at support@skillnavigator.com or by filling out the contact form on the "Help" page.
